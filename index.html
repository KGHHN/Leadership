📚 Hofstede → Maak Responsible Leadership

Power Distance (70–80)
→ Servant Leadership: silence is cultural, not consent — use 1-on-1s & anonymous channels.
Individualism vs. Collectivism
→ Citizen Leadership: collectivists need relationship first; individualists need directness.
Achievement (MAS)
→ Weaver Leadership: connect worker care and verified claims — not opposites.
Uncertainty Avoidance (60–65)
→ Visionary Leadership: clear roadmaps & timelines build trust.

🌀 Meta-Lesson: Lead Contextually, Not Linearly

NordWear's crises are non-linear — interconnected and unpredictable, so no single fixed plan solves them. "Since complex problems in VUCA contexts are non-linear ... they ought not to be approached linearly" (Plowman & Duchon, 2008). Anna must lead contextually: same values, different behaviour per culture — that is cultural intelligence.
